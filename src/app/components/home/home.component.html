Specialized IT Training

Generation Italy excels in providing specialized training programs in the field of information technology. The organization is dedicated to equipping young individuals between the ages of 18 and 29 with comprehensive and industry-relevant skills in the ever-evolving IT landscape.

Tailored Programs for Youth Development

One of Generation Italy's strengths lies in its ability to tailor training programs specifically designed for the development of young talents. The organization recognizes the unique needs and aspirations of individuals in the 18-29 age group, creating educational initiatives that not only build technical expertise but also foster personal and professional growth.

Hands-On Learning Approach

Generation Italy stands out for its hands-on learning approach, providing participants with practical, real-world experiences in addition to theoretical knowledge. The organization believes in the importance of practical skills application, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared to meet the demands of the IT industry upon completing their training.

ABOUT US

Generation Italy helps young individuals develop highly qualified skills, providing businesses with the professional profiles they need. Our mission is to enable young people to build their careers, aiding them in developing highly qualified skills and supplying businesses with motivated professional profiles. In Italy, where over a million youths are unemployed, our pragmatic approach creates value for employers by connecting skills demand, required professional expertise, and regions with high unemployment rates. Our goal is to offer free training courses and support young individuals in entering the workforce, contributing to reducing unemployment.

Our vision is to ensure employment that initiates the professional careers of every young person worldwide. Generation focuses on reducing youth unemployment by providing essential skills, making otherwise inaccessible careers accessible and supporting companies in finding the professionals they need.

For further information, contact us at info.italy@generation.org.
